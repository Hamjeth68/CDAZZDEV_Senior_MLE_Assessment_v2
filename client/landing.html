Equity Research Assistant

A fintech prototype that turns price data, news sentiment, and AI agents into research-ready insights for investment analysts and strategic teams.

Open interactive dashboard View core features

Core product strengths

Fast equity research

Generate analyst-style briefing summaries from ticker data, market indicators, and curated news in a single workflow.

Sentiment-driven signals

Combine fine-tuned sentiment classification with market data to surface directional confidence and risk context.

Agentic report synthesis

Use a two-agent architecture to blend quantitative analysis and qualitative research into readable investment narratives.

Customer benefits

For investment analysts

Reduce manual research time by quickly validating price momentum and news sentiment before building a recommendation.

For fintech hiring

Showcase a working AI-driven workflow that spans backend engineering, machine learning, and frontend product design.

For data science portfolios

Demonstrate how structured financial features and LLM outputs can be combined into a trustworthy, explainable product.

How to launch this prototype

Use the interactive dashboard locally for the full experience. The marketing page is ideal for public portfolio hosting, while the Python backend runs the live analysis.

The dashboard includes sample ticker quick actions, recent query recall, and exportable research summaries to make demonstrations concrete.

python app_server.py --port 8000

Open http://127.0.0.1:8000 in your browser

For free public deployment, host this marketing page on GitHub Pages and offer the interactive app as a local demo or a free-host proof of concept.
